On a Quest for Impact

I am a dedicated researcher and educator with a robust academic foundation in Geography, Climate Change, and Sustainable Development. With hands-on experience in leveraging Copernicus data and technologies, I am passionate about applying my technical and analytical expertise to drive initiatives focused on climate resilience and sustainable development. My goal is to contribute innovative solutions that bridge the gap between geospatial research and actionable strategies for a more sustainable future.
